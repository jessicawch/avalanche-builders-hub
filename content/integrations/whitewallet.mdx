## Overview

Whitewallet is a secure, non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet that enables users to manage, swap, and explore Web3 assets across multiple blockchain networks. With support for Ethereum, Avalanche, Solana, Arbitrum, Whitechain, BNB Chain, Optimism, Tron, TON, and other major blockchains, the wallet provides a unified interface for cross-chain asset management. Built with self-custody principles, users maintain full control of their private keys and digital assets, ensuring security and autonomy in their Web3 interactions.

## Features

- **Multi-Chain Support**: Store, send, receive, and manage crypto assets across Ethereum, Avalanche, Solana, Arbitrum, Whitechain, BNB Chain, Optimism, Tron, TON, and other supported blockchains from a single interface.

- **Cross-Chain Swaps**: Execute asset swaps between supported networks directly within the wallet application, eliminating the need for external bridges or additional tools.

- **Non-Custodial Security**: Maintain full control of your private keys with true self-custody architecture. Your funds remain secure and accessible only to you, with no risk of account freezes or third-party access.

- **Multi-Asset Management**: Support for leading digital assets including Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), USD Coin (USDC), AVAX, WBT, DAI, BNB, TRX, TON, OP, XPL, ARB, and many other tokens across supported networks.

- **User-Friendly Interface**: Streamlined token management and portfolio tracking designed for both beginners and experienced users, with intuitive transaction flows and clear asset visibility.

- **Web3 Integration**: Connect seamlessly to decentralized applications and DeFi protocols directly from the wallet, enabling interaction with the broader Web3 ecosystem.
